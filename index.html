THE PRESIDENT PAUL BIYA

Who is Paul Biya?

Paul Biya is the president of Cameroon since 1982.He is currrently 93 years old and is the oldest head of state in the world.

Who was he before presidency?

  • He was born in a village in the south region of cameroon
  • He attended local school before studying in france
  • He offered political science and public law in college

How long has he ruled?

The president Biya has ruled for a longtime. His numerous elections have all been a win. From 1984 up to 2026 has all been wins although opposition parties and international groups have frequently complained about electoral fraud.

What has he done for his people?

He has done plenty of good deeds for his people. Those include:

  • Expanded access to public schools
  • Infrastructure and economic development
  • National unity and conflict
